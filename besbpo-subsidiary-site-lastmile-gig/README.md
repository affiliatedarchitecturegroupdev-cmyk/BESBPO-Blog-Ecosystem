# besbpo-subsidiary-site-lastmile-gig

Phase 4 pilot subsidiary site for Lastmile Gig (Pty) Ltd, forked from
`besbpo-subsidiary-site-template`. Implements `BESBPO-BLOG-ARCH-05`
Phase 4 (Subsidiary Pilot Wave).

- **Division:** `last-mile-services` (secondary: `logistics`)
- **Delivery mode:** `client_side`, `timeline` placement
- **Brand:** dark theme with an electric-blue accent, reflecting the
  platform/AI positioning (AI Singularity OS) rather than a traditional
  logistics look — deliberately distinct from Garlaws' industrial
  navy/amber pilot even though both share the `logistics` tag, showing
  the syndication contract is brand-agnostic on the receiving end.
